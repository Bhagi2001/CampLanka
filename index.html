About CampLanka

Founded in 2020, CampLanka is Sri Lanka's premier camping equipment rental service. We're passionate about making outdoor adventures accessible to everyone, from weekend warriors to seasoned explorers.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable camping gear that enhances your connection with Sri Lanka's stunning natural landscapes. Whether you're planning a romantic getaway to the hill country or a family adventure in our national parks, we have the equipment you need.
